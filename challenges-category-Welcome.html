Welcome to IrisCTF 2025! As usual, our team has been working hard to make this event happen and we're excited to be bringing these challenges to you. Whether you're a total newbie or a seasoned pro, we hope you'll have fun and learn something new!


This year, we're excited to launch a new CTF platform theme. You can switch between the CTFx and WebGL platform themes at any time. If you're on WebGL, hit "CTFx" at the top of your screen. If you're on CTFx, you can switch to WebGL using the link at the bottom of the page. We recommend CTFx if you run into problems with the WebGL or are on older hardware.


To make sure you know how to switch between the two, go ahead and submit the two halves of the flag found in the "About" page of each site.
